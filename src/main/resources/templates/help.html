ABOUT
Similar in gameplay and structure to the great classic board game, New Monopoly® connects players all over the world in a live, multiplayer game experience. Hasbro currently does not have a free virtual board game, nor do they offer a balanced board game experience. Often players at a strategic advantage dominate the game. This online game model will allow players to experience a more difficult, but balanced gameplay with the following added features:

 Seasons that change the Luck and Charity cards, the board graphics, and the property values.

 Options that allow randomized board spaces, or other game board styles.

 House rules that turn a boring old board game into a personalized experience.

Everyone plays the game a little differently; however, with the help of New Monopoly®, the style of the traditional board game will change forever. Each experience with New Monopoly® will give players a unique sense of familiarity, yet the challenges are far greater. Sign up now, and help us launch New Monopoly® into the future of gaming!
