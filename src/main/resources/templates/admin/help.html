ABOUT
Similar in gameplay and structure to the great classic board game, New Monopoly® connects players all over the world in a live, multiplayer game experience. Hasbro currently does not have a free virtual board game, nor do they offer a balanced board game experience. Often players at a strategic advantage dominate the game. This online game model will allow players to experience a more difficult, but balanced gameplay with the following added features:

 Seasons that change the Luck and Charity cards, the board graphics, and the property values.

 Options that allow randomized board spaces, or other game board styles.

 House rules that turn a boring old board game into a personalized experience.

Everyone plays the game a little differently; however, with the help of New Monopoly®, the style of the traditional board game will change forever. Each experience with New Monopoly® will give players a unique sense of familiarity, yet the challenges are far greater. Sign up now, and help us launch New Monopoly® into the future of gaming!
HOW TO PLAY:
CREATE/JOIN GAME
Select Create/Join Game.

 Selecting the CREATE GAME button: Allows a player to become the host player; this player will determine the web-based game settings unique to New Monopoly®. The host player sets the lobby to public or private (password required), decides the difficulty level, chooses the number of allowed players and the number of turns to be played.

 Selecting the JOIN GAME button: Allows non-host players to search for all available games to join. These players will also be able to filter these games out based on difficulty level, number of players, and number of turns; as well as search for a game by a specific ID. If players desire to enter a private lobby, they must have the password required to gain access in that lobby.
HOW TO PLAY:
WINNING/LOSING
New Monopoly® has the same concepts of winning and losing from the traditional game.

 Winning the game: The object of the game is to be the only player remaining (who has not gone bankrupt).

 Losing the game: The losers of the game are the players to go “bankrupt,” or lose all money and property.
HOW TO PLAY:
BEGINNING GAME
New Monopoly® has the same concepts of starting a game from the traditional game.

 Starting the game: To determine which player will roll first, each player will roll the dice once. The player who rolls the highest number begins the game. Each subsequent turn will be based on the player’s rank during the initial dice roll.
HOW TO PLAY:
PLAYING THE GAME
After the initial roll is determined, the player pawns will move across the board counter-clockwise.

 Each turn a player passes START, the player receives $200 from THE BANK.

 Property spaces: When a player lands on an unowned property space, the player has the option to BUY or AUCTION the property. To BUY the property, the player must pay the full amount listed on the property card; to AUCTION the property, the bank will take the property and sell it to the highest bidder (out of the rest of the players). Once a player owns all property cards of the same color group, this player owns a MONOPOLY, and can begin building HOUSES/HOTELS to collect higher rent. Players also have the option to purchase RAILROADS and UTILITIES. RAILROADS are bought and auctioned the same way as properties. The amount in rent a player receives from an Railroad space depends on how many Railroads the player owns. UTILITIES are bought and auctioned the same way as properties. The amount in rent a player receives from a utility space is five times the amount shown on the dice roll.
HOW TO PLAY:
PLAYING THE GAME
 Two or more paws may land on the same space; however, if the first pawn to land on the space has purchased the property, the second pawn will owe the first pawn RENT.

 Building HOUSES/HOTELS increases rent on monopolized properties. To build HOUSES/HOTELS, a player must own all properties of one color group. Players must build evenly when purchasing houses and hotels: a player cannot build two houses on a property space until all property spaces have one house. To buy a hotel, a player must have four houses on each property space of the same color group: a player cannot build more than one hotel per property.

 Players can SELL/TRADE properties, Railroads, or utilities.

 Players can make extra money by selling houses/hotels, selling properties, Railroads, or utilities, or mortgaging property. Each property has a mortgage amount listed below the selling price. To repay a mortgage, a player must pay the original mortgage amount, plus 10% interest.
HOW TO PLAY:
PLAYING THE GAME
 LUCK and CHARITY spaces: When a player lands on a LUCK or CHARITY space, the player will see a pop-up card with the instructions. These cards can bring both good and bad fortune. If a player receives a BAIL card, he/she can use it in future turns to get out of jail for free (or sell it, if the player needs extra cash).

 HOUSE RULES space: This can be a “free” space, in which players do not win or lose any money by landing here, or the host player can one of dozens of house rules to further personalize the game experience. These options include the popular “lottery” option, in which money that players owe to income taxes and other expenses is combined into a lottery; the first player to land on the House Rule space will receive all money collected in the lottery.

 BUSTED and LOCKED-UP spaces: When a player lands on the Go-to- Jail corner, the player will see a pop-up that says BUSTED. The player will go directly to the Jail space. The player can get out of jail by paying a $50 fine, using a BAIL card, or rolling a double on the dice. When a player lands on the Jail corner, the player is just visiting, and it is considered a “free” space.
